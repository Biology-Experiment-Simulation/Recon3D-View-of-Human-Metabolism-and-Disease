CC   -!- FUNCTION: RNA-binding protein that binds to iron-responsive
CC       elements (IRES), which are stem-loop structures found in the 5'-
CC       UTR of ferritin, and delta aminolevulinic acid synthase mRNAs, and
CC       in the 3'-UTR of transferrin receptor mRNA. Binding to the IRE
CC       element in ferritin results in the repression of its mRNA
CC       translation. Binding of the protein to the transferrin receptor
CC       mRNA inhibits the degradation of this otherwise rapidly degraded
CC       mRNA. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:7983023}.
