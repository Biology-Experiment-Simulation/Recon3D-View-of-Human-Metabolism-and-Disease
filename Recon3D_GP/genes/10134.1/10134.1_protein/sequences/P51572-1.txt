CC   -!- FUNCTION: Functions as a chaperone protein. Is one of the most
CC       abundant endoplasmic reticulum (ER) proteins. Plays a role in the
CC       export of secreted proteins in the ER, the recognition of
CC       abnormally folded protein and their targeting to the ER
CC       associated-degradation (ERAD). Also serves as a cargo receptor for
CC       the export of transmembrane proteins. May be involved in CASP8-
CC       mediated apoptosis. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10958671,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:18287538, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9396746}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer and heterodimer with BCAP29. Binds CASP8
CC       (isoform 9) as a complex containing BCAP31, BCAP29, BCL2 and/or
CC       BCL2L1. Interacts with VAMP3, VAMP1 and membrane IgD
CC       immunoglobulins. May interact with ACTG1 and non-muscle myosin II.
CC       Interacts with HACD2 (PubMed:15024066).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11917123, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15024066,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:23967155, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9334338}.
