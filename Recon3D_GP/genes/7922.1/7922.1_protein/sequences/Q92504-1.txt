CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Endoplasmic reticulum membrane; Multi-pass

CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Widely expressed.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:14525538, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15705588}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Down-regulated by Zn(+2).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15705588}.
CC   -!- PTM: Rapidly phosphorylated by CK2 following Zn(2+) treatment.

CC       release. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22317921}.
