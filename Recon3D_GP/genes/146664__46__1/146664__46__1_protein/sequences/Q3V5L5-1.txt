CC   -!- FUNCTION: Glycosyltransferase that acts on alpha-linked mannose of
CC       N-glycans and O-mannosyl glycans. Catalyzes the transfer of N-
CC       acetylglucosamine (GlcNAc) to the beta 1-6 linkage of the mannose
CC       residue of GlcNAcbeta1,2-Manalpha on both the alpha1,3- and
CC       alpha1,6-linked mannose arms in the core structure of N-glycan.
CC       Also acts on the GlcNAcbeta1,2-Manalpha1-Ser/Thr moiety, forming a
CC       2,6-branched structure in brain O-mannosyl glycan. Plays an active
CC       role in modulating integrin and laminin-dependent adhesion and
CC       migration of neuronal cells via its activity in the O-mannosyl
CC       glycan pathway. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12941944,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:14617637, ECO:0000269|PubMed:14623122,
