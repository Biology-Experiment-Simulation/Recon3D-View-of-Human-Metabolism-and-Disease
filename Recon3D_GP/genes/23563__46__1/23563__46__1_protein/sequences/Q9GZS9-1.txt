CC   -!- FUNCTION: Sulfotransferase that utilizes 3'-phospho-5'-adenylyl
CC       sulfate (PAPS) as sulfonate donor to catalyze the transfer of
CC       sulfate to position 6 of non-reducing N-acetylglucosamine (GlcNAc)
CC       residues and O-linked sugars of mucin-type acceptors. Acts on the
CC       non-reducing terminal GlcNAc of short carbohydrate substrates.
CC       However, it does not transfer sulfate to longer carbohydrate
CC       substrates that have poly-N-acetyllactosamine structures. Has no
CC       activity toward keratan. Not involved in generating HEV-expressed
CC       ligands for SELL. Its substrate specificity may be influenced by
CC       its subcellular location. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10491328,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:11352640, ECO:0000269|PubMed:12218059,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12626414}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Golgi apparatus membrane
CC       {ECO:0000305|PubMed:12855678}; Single-pass type II membrane
CC       protein {ECO:0000305|PubMed:12855678}. Note=Golgi membrane, early
CC       secretory pathway.
