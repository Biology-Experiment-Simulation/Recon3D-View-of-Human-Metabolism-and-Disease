CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homotetramer. {ECO:0000255|HAMAP-Rule:MF_03195,
CC       ECO:0000269|Ref.8}.
CC   -!- POLYMORPHISM: At least two different alleles are known.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the IMPDH/GMPR family. GuaC type 1
CC       subfamily. {ECO:0000255|HAMAP-Rule:MF_03195}.

CC       reductase, and PubMed:1694726 showed that the chimeric protein is
CC       an artifact. {ECO:0000305|PubMed:2758468}.
