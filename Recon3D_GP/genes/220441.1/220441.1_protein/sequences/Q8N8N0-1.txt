CC       polyubiquitination of RRAGA in response to amino acid starvation.

CC       cellular response to amino acid availability (PubMed:25936802).
CC       Also mediates 'Lys-48'-linked polyubiquitination of target
CC       proteins and their subsequent targeting to the proteasome for
CC       degradation. Induces apoptosis when overexpressed
CC       (PubMed:21203937). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21203937,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:25936802}.
