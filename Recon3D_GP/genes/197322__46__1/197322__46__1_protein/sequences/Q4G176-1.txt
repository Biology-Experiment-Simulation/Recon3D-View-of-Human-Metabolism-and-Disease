CC       [MIM:614265]: A metabolic disease characterized by malonic and

CC       of normal malonyl-CoA decarboxylase activity. Clinical features
CC       include coma, ketoacidosis, hypoglycemia, failure to thrive,
CC       microcephaly, dystonia, axial hypotonia and/or developmental
CC       delay, and neurologic manifestations including seizures,
