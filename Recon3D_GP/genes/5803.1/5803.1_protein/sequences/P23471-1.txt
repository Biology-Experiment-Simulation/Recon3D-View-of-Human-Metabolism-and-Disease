CC   -!- FUNCTION: Protein tyrosine phosphatase that negatively regulates
CC       oligodendrocyte precursor proliferation in the embryonic spinal
CC       cord. Required for normal differentiation of the precursor cells
CC       into mature, fully myelinating oligodendrocytes. May play a role
CC       in protecting oligondendrocytes against apoptosis. May play a role
CC       in the establishment of contextual memory, probably via the
CC       dephosphorylation of proteins that are part of important signaling
CC       cascades (By similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Protein tyrosine phosphate + H(2)O = protein
CC       tyrosine + phosphate. {ECO:0000255|PROSITE-ProRule:PRU10044}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: The carbonic-anhydrase like domain interacts with CNTN1
CC       (contactin) (PubMed:20133774). Interacts with PTN
CC       (PubMed:16814777). Interaction with PTN promotes formation of
CC       homooligomers; oligomerization impairs phosphatase activity (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q62656,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:16814777, ECO:0000269|PubMed:20133774}.
