CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalytic histone acetyltransferase subunit of the RNA
CC       polymerase II elongator complex, which is a component of the RNA
CC       polymerase II (Pol II) holoenzyme and is involved in
CC       transcriptional elongation. Elongator may play a role in chromatin
CC       remodeling and is involved in acetylation of histones H3 and
CC       probably H4. Involved in acetylation of alpha-tubulin
CC       (PubMed:19185337). May also have a methyltransferase activity.
CC       Involved in cell migration. Involved in neurogenesis. Regulates
CC       the migration and branching of projection neurons in the
CC       developing cerebral cortex, through a process depending on alpha-
CC       tubulin acetylation. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9CZX0,
