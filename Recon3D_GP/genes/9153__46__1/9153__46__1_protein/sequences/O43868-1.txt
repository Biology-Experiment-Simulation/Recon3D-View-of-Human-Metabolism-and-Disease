CC   -!- FUNCTION: Sodium-dependent and purine-selective transporter.
CC       Exhibits the transport characteristics of the nucleoside transport
CC       system cif or N1 subtype (N1/cif) (selective for purine
CC       nucleosides and uridine). Plays a critical role in specific uptake
CC       and salvage of purine nucleosides in kidney and other tissues.
CC   -!- ENZYME REGULATION: Inhibited by formycin B.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Membrane; Multi-pass membrane protein.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in heart and skeletal muscle
CC       followed by liver, kidney, intestine, pancreas, placenta and
CC       brain. Weak expression in lung.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the concentrative nucleoside transporter
CC       (CNT) (TC 2.A.41) family. {ECO:0000305}.
