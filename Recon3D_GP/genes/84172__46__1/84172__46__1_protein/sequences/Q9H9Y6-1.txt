CC       of DNA into RNA using the four ribonucleoside triphosphates as
CC       substrates. Second largest core component of RNA polymerase I
CC       which synthesizes ribosomal RNA precursors. Proposed to contribute
CC       to the polymerase catalytic activity and forms the polymerase
CC       active center together with the largest subunit. Pol I is composed
CC       of mobile elements and RPA2 is part of the core element with the
CC       central large cleft and probably a clamp element that moves to
CC       open and close the cleft (By similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
