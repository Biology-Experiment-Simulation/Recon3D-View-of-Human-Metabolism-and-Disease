CC       progressive tubulo-interstitial kidney disorder histologically

CC       the basement membrane, interstitial fibrosis and, in the advanced
CC       stages, medullary cysts. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18199800}. Note=The

CC       this entry.
CC   -!- DISEASE: Renal-hepatic-pancreatic dysplasia 2 (RHPD2)
CC       [MIM:615415]: A form of renal-hepatic-pancreatic dysplasia, a
CC       disease characterized by cystic malformations of the kidneys,
CC       liver, and pancreas. The pathological findings consist of
CC       multicystic dysplastic kidneys, dilated and dysgenetic bile ducts,
CC       a dysplastic pancreas with dilated ducts, cysts, fibrosis and
CC       inflammatory infiltrates. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:23418306}. Note=The
CC       disease is caused by mutations affecting the gene represented in
CC       this entry.
