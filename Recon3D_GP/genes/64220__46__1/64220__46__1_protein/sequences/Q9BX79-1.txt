CC   -!- FUNCTION: May act as a high-affinity cell-surface receptor for the
CC       complex retinol-retinol binding protein (RBP/RBP4). Acts by
CC       removing retinol from RBP/RBP4 and transports it across the plasma
CC       membrane, where it can be metabolized. This mechanism does not
CC       depend on endocytosis. Binds to RBP/RBP4 with high affinity.
CC       Increases cellular retinol uptake from the retinol-RBP complex (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
