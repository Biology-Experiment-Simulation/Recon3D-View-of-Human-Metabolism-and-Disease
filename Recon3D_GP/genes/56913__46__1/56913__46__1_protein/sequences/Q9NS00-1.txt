CC   -!- FUNCTION: Glycosyltransferase that generates the core 1 O-glycan
CC       Gal-beta1-3GalNAc-alpha1-Ser/Thr (T antigen), which is a precursor
CC       for many extended O-glycans in glycoproteins. Plays a central role
CC       in many processes, such as angiogenesis, thrombopoiesis and kidney
CC       homeostasis development.
