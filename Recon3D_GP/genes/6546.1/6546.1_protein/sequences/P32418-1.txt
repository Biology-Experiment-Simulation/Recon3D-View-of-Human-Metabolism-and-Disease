CC   -!- FUNCTION: Mediates the exchange of one Ca(2+) ion against three to
CC       four Na(+) ions across the cell membrane, and thereby contributes
CC       to the regulation of cytoplasmic Ca(2+) levels and Ca(2+)-
CC       dependent cellular processes (PubMed:1374913, PubMed:11241183,
CC       PubMed:1476165). Contributes to Ca(2+) transport during
CC       excitation-contraction coupling in muscle. In a first phase,
CC       voltage-gated channels mediate the rapid increase of cytoplasmic
CC       Ca(2+) levels due to release of Ca(2+) stores from the endoplasmic
CC       reticulum. SLC8A1 mediates the export of Ca(2+) from the cell
CC       during the next phase, so that cytoplasmic Ca(2+) levels rapidly
CC       return to baseline. Required for normal embryonic heart
CC       development and the onset of heart contractions.
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P70414, ECO:0000269|PubMed:11241183,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:1374913, ECO:0000269|PubMed:1476165}.
CC   -!- ENZYME REGULATION: Activated by micromolar levels of Ca(2+).
CC       {ECO:0000305}.

CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:1374913, ECO:0000269|PubMed:1476165}; Multi-
