CC       trivalent arsenicals producing methylated and dimethylated
CC       arsenicals. It methylates arsenite to form methylarsonate, Me-
CC       AsO(3)H(2), which is reduced by methylarsonate reductase to
CC       methylarsonite, Me-As(OH)2. Methylarsonite is also a substrate and
CC       it is converted into the much less toxic compound dimethylarsinate
CC       (cacodylate), Me(2)As(O)-OH (By similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
