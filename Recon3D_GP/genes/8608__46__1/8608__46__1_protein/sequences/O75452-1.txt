CC   -!- FUNCTION: Oxidoreductase with a preference for NAD. Oxidizes all-
CC       trans-retinol and 13-cis-retinol to the corresponding aldehydes.
CC       Has higher activity towards CRBP-bound retinol than with free
CC       retinol. Oxidizes 3-alpha-hydroxysteroids. Oxidizes androstanediol
CC       and androsterone to dihydrotestosterone and androstanedione. Can
CC       also catalyze the reverse reaction. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10329026,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12534290, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9677409}.
CC   -!- ENZYME REGULATION: Inhibited by citral, perillyl alcohol,
