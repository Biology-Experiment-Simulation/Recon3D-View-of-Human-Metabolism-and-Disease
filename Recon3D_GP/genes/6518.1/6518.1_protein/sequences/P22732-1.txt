CC   -!- FUNCTION: Functions as a fructose transporter that has only low
CC       activity with other monosaccharides (PubMed:8333543). Can mediate
CC       the uptake of 2-deoxyglucose, but with low efficiency
CC       (PubMed:1695905). Essential for fructose uptake in the small
CC       intestine. Plays a role in the regulation of salt uptake and blood
CC       pressure in response to dietary fructose. Required for the
CC       development of high blood pressure in response to high dietary
CC       fructose intake (By similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9WV38,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:1695905, ECO:0000269|PubMed:8333543}.
