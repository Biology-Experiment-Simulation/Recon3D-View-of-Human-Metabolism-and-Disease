CC       group Lewis determination; Lewis-positive (Le(+)) individuals have
CC       an active enzyme while Lewis-negative (Le(-)) individuals have an
CC       inactive enzyme. Also acts on the corresponding 1,4-galactosyl
CC       derivative, forming 1,3-L-fucosyl links.
