CC   -!- DISEASE: Barrett esophagus (BE) [MIM:614266]: A condition
CC       characterized by a metaplastic change in which normal esophageal
CC       squamous epithelium is replaced by a columnar and intestinal-type
CC       epithelium. Patients with Barrett esophagus have an increased risk
CC       of esophageal adenocarcinoma. The main cause of Barrett esophagus
CC       is gastroesophageal reflux. The retrograde movement of acid and
CC       bile salts from the stomach into the esophagus causes prolonged
CC       injury to the esophageal epithelium and induces chronic
CC       esophagitis, which in turn is believed to trigger the pathologic
CC       changes. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22157330}. Note=The disease is caused
CC       by mutations affecting the gene represented in this entry. The
CC       pathologic mechanisms leading to Barrett esophagus involve GPX7
CC       dysfunction that results in higher levels of hydrogen peroxide and
CC       ROS-induced oxidative stress and DNA damage in esophageal cells.
