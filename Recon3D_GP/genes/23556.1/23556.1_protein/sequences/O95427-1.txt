CC       ethanolamine phosphate to the first alpha-1,4-linked mannose of
CC       the glycosylphosphatidylinositol precursor of GPI-anchor (By

CC       chromosome missegregation. {ECO:0000250,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:23446422}.

CC   -!- DISEASE: Multiple congenital anomalies-hypotonia-seizures syndrome
CC       1 (MCAHS1) [MIM:614080]: An autosomal recessive disorder
CC       characterized by neonatal hypotonia, lack of psychomotor
CC       development, seizures, dysmorphic features, and variable
CC       congenital anomalies involving the cardiac, urinary, and
CC       gastrointestinal systems. Most affected individuals die before 3
CC       years of age. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21493957}. Note=The disease is
CC       caused by mutations affecting the gene represented in this entry.
