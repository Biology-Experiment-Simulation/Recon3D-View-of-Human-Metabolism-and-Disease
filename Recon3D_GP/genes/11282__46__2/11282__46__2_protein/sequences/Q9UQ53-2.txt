CC   -!- FUNCTION: Glycosyltransferase that participates in the transfer of
CC       N-acetylglucosamine (GlcNAc) to the core mannose residues of N-
CC       linked glycans. Catalyzes the formation of the GlcNAcbeta1-4
CC       branch on the GlcNAcbeta1-2Manalpha1-3 arm of the core structure
CC       of N-linked glycans. Essential for the production of tri- and
CC       tetra-antennary N-linked sugar chains. Has lower affinities for
CC       donors or acceptors than MGAT4A, suggesting that, under
CC       physiological conditions, it is not the main contributor in N-
CC       glycan biosynthesis.
