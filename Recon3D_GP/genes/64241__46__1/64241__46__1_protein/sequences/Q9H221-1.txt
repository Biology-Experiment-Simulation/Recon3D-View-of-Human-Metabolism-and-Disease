CC       hypercholesterolemia, very high levels of plant sterols in the
CC       plasma, and frequently develop tendon and tuberous xanthomas,
CC       accelerated atherosclerosis and premature coronary artery disease.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11099417, ECO:0000269|PubMed:11452359}.
