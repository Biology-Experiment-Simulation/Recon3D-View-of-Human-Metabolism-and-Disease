CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Ubiquitous. Highly expressed in spleen and
CC       leukocytes. Generally the expression of isoform 1 is higher than
CC       that of isoform 2. Isoform 2 is particularly abundant in testis
CC       and brain. In testis, isoform 1 is detected in spermatogonia at
CC       different stages and absent from Sertoli cells, while isoform 2 is
CC       present in both (at protein level). Expressed in the choroid
CC       plexus epithelium, isoform 1 being localized at the basolateral
CC       membrane and isoform 2 at the apical one, as well as in the
CC       subapical intracellular vesicular compartments. Differential
CC       expression of both isoforms is also observed in other brain
CC       region: isoform 1 is very abundant in the gray matter of the
CC       frontal cortex, but is not associated with neuronal cell bodies.
CC       Not detected in the white matter. In contrast, isoform 2 is
CC       associated with neuronal bodies and axons in both the gray and the
CC       white matters of the frontal cortex.
