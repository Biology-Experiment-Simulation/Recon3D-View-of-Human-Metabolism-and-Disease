CC       rT3. Other potential substrates, such as triiodothyronine (T3),
CC       17-beta-glucuronosyl estradiol, estrone-3-sulfate and
CC       sulfobromophthalein (BSP) are transported with much lower
CC       efficiency. May play a signifiant role in regulating T4 flux into
CC       and out of the brain (By similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
