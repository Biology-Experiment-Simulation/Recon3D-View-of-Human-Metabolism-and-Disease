CC   -!- FUNCTION: Polypeptide N-acetylgalactosaminyltransferase that
CC       catalyzes the initiation of protein O-linked glycosylation and is
CC       involved in left/right asymmetry by mediating O-glycosylation of
CC       NOTCH1. O-glycosylation of NOTCH1 promotes activation of NOTCH1,
CC       modulating the balance between motile and immotile (sensory) cilia
CC       at the left-right organiser (LRO). Polypeptide N-
CC       acetylgalactosaminyltransferases catalyze the transfer of an N-
CC       acetyl-D-galactosamine residue to a serine or threonine residue on
CC       the protein receptor. Displays the same enzyme activity toward
CC       MUC1, MUC4, and EA2 than GALNT1. Not involved in glycosylation of
CC       erythropoietin (EPO). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11925450,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:24226769}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: UDP-N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosamine +
