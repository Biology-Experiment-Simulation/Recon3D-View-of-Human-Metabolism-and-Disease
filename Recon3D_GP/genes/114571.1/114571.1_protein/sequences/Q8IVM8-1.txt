CC   -!- FUNCTION: Sodium-independent organic anion transporter which
CC       exhibits high specificity for sulfated conjugates of xenobiotics
CC       and steroid hormones. It is also specifically activated by 3 to 5
CC       carbons-containing short-chain fatty acids/SCFAs, including
CC       propionate, butyrate and valerate. May operate the exchange of
CC       sulfated organic components against short-chain fatty acids/SCFAs
CC       at the sinusoidal membrane of hepatocytes.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17393504}.
