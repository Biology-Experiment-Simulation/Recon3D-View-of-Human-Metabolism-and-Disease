CC       gait disturbances, ataxia, dystonia, and spasticity, which in some
CC       cases may result in loss of ability to walk. Cognitive function is
CC       usually preserved, although mildly delayed development has been

CC       metabolic decompensation. Some patients may have recovery of some
CC       neurologic deficits. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22152682}. Note=The
