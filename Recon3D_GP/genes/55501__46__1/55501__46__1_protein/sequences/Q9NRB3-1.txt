CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the transfer of sulfate to position 4 of the
CC       N-acetylgalactosamine (GalNAc) residue of chondroitin and
CC       desulfated dermatan sulfate. Chondroitin sulfate constitutes the
CC       predominant proteoglycan present in cartilage and is distributed
CC       on the surfaces of many cells and extracellular matrices. Activity
CC       toward partially desulfated dermatan sulfate is however lower.
CC       Does not form 4, 6-di-O-sulfated GalNAc when chondroitin sulfate C
CC       is used as an acceptor.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: 3'-phosphoadenylyl sulfate + chondroitin =
CC       adenosine 3',5'-bisphosphate + chondroitin 4'-sulfate.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10781601}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Golgi apparatus membrane {ECO:0000250};
CC       Single-pass type II membrane protein {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Widely expressed. Expressed a high level in
CC       spinal chord, heart, spleen, thyroid, pituitary gland, adrenal
CC       gland, peripheral blood leukocytes, thymus, lung, small intestine,
CC       fetal kidney, fetal spleen and fetal lung.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10781601}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the sulfotransferase 2 family.
