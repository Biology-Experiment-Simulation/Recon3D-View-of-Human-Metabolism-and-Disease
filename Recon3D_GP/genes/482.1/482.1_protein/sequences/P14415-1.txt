CC   -!- FUNCTION: This is the non-catalytic component of the active
CC       enzyme, which catalyzes the hydrolysis of ATP coupled with the
CC       exchange of Na(+) and K(+) ions across the plasma membrane. The
CC       exact function of the beta-2 subunit is not known.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: Mediates cell adhesion of neurons and astrocytes, and
CC       promotes neurite outgrowth. {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Composed of three subunits: alpha (catalytic), beta and
CC       gamma.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane; Single-pass type II membrane
CC       protein.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The C-terminal lobe folds into an immunoglobulin-like
CC       domain and mediates cell adhesion properties. {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the X(+)/potassium ATPases subunit beta
CC       family. {ECO:0000305}.
