CC   -!- FUNCTION: Antiporter transporting nucleotide sugars such as UDP-N-
CC       acetylglucosamine (UDP-GlcNAc), UDP-glucose (UDP-Glc) and GDP-
CC       mannose (GDP-Man) pooled in the cytosol into the lumen of the
CC       Golgi in exchange for the corresponding nucleosides monophosphates
CC       (UMP for UDP-sugars and GMP for GDP-sugars). May take part in
CC       heparan sulfate synthesis by supplying UDP-Glc-NAc, the donor
CC       substrate, and thus be involved in growth factor signaling.
