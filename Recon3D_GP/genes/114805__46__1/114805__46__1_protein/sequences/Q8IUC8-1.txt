CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the initial reaction in O-linked
CC       oligosaccharide biosynthesis, the transfer of an N-acetyl-D-
CC       galactosamine residue to a serine or threonine residue on the
CC       protein receptor. Has a much stronger activity than GALNT1 to
CC       transfer GalNAc to mucin peptides, such as Muc5Ac and Muc7. Able
CC       to glycosylate SDC3. May be responsible for the synthesis of Tn
CC       antigen in neuronal cells.
