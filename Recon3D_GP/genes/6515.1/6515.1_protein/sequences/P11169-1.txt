CC   -!- FUNCTION: Facilitative glucose transporter that can also mediate
CC       the uptake of various other monosaccharides across the cell
CC       membrane (PubMed:9477959, PubMed:26176916). Mediates the uptake of
CC       glucose, 2-deoxyglucose, galactose, mannose, xylose and fucose,
CC       and probably also dehydroascorbate (PubMed:9477959,
CC       PubMed:26176916). Does not mediate fructose transport
CC       (PubMed:9477959, PubMed:26176916). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:26176916,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9477959}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane {ECO:0000269|PubMed:9477959,
CC       ECO:0000305|PubMed:26176916}; Multi-pass membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:26176916}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Highly expressed in brain. Expressed in many
CC       tissues. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:3170580}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: Transport is mediated via a series of conformation
CC       changes, switching between a conformation where the substrate-
CC       binding cavity is accessible from the outside, and a another
CC       conformation where it is accessible from the cytoplasm.
CC       {ECO:0000305|PubMed:26176916}.
