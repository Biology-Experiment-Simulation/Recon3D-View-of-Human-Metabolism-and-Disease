CC   -!- DISEASE: Mitochondrial phosphoenolpyruvate carboxykinase
CC       deficiency (M-PEPCKD) [MIM:261650]: Metabolic disorder resulting
CC       from impaired gluconeogenesis. It is a rare disease with less than
CC       10 cases reported in the literature. Clinical characteristics
CC       include hypotonia, hepatomegaly, failure to thrive, lactic
CC       acidosis and hypoglycemia. Autopsy reveals fatty infiltration of
CC       both the liver and kidneys. The disorder is transmitted as an
CC       autosomal recessive trait. Note=The disease is caused by mutations
