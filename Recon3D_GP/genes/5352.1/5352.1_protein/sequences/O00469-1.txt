CC   -!- DISEASE: Bruck syndrome 2 (BRKS2) [MIM:609220]: An autosomal
CC       recessive disease characterized by generalized osteopenia,
CC       congenital joint contractures, fragile bones with onset of
CC       fractures in infancy or early childhood, short stature, severe
CC       limb deformity, progressive scoliosis, and pterygia. It is
CC       distinguished from osteogenesis imperfecta by the absence of
CC       hearing loss and dentinogenesis imperfecta, and by the presence of
CC       clubfoot and congenital joint limitations.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12881513, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15523624}.
CC       Note=The disease is caused by mutations affecting the gene
CC       represented in this entry. The molecular defect leading to Bruck
CC       syndrome is an aberrant cross-linking of bone collagen, due to
CC       underhydroxylation of lysine residues within the telopeptides of
CC       type I collagen, whereas the lysine residues in the triple helix
CC       are normal.
