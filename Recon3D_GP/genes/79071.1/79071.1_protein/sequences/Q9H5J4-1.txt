CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the first and rate-limiting reaction of the
CC       four that constitute the long-chain fatty acids elongation cycle.
CC       This endoplasmic reticulum-bound enzymatic process, allows the
CC       addition of 2 carbons to the chain of long- and very long-chain
CC       fatty acids/VLCFAs per cycle. Condensing enzyme that elongates
CC       fatty acids with 12, 14 and 16 carbons with higher activity toward
CC       C16:0 acyl-CoAs. Catalyzes the synthesis of unsaturated C16 long
CC       chain fatty acids and, to a lesser extent, C18:0 and those with
CC       low desaturation degree. May participate in the production of
CC       saturated and monounsaturated VLCFAs of different chain lengths
CC       that are involved in multiple biological processes as precursors
CC       of membrane lipids and lipid mediators. {ECO:0000255|HAMAP-
CC       Rule:MF_03206, ECO:0000269|PubMed:20937905}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: A very-long-chain acyl-CoA + malonyl-CoA = CoA
