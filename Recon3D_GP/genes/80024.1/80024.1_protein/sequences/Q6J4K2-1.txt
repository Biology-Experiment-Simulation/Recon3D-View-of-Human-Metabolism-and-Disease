CC   -!- FUNCTION: Mitochondrial sodium/calcium antiporter that mediates
CC       sodium-dependent calcium efflux from mitochondrion, thereby acting
CC       as a key regulator of mitochondrion calcium homeostasis. Regulates
CC       rates of glucose-dependent insulin secretion in pancreatic beta-
CC       cells during the first phase of insulin secretion: acts by
CC       mediating efflux of calcium from mitochondrion, thereby affecting
CC       cytoplasmic calcium responses. Able to transport Ca(2+) in
CC       exchange of either Li(+) or Na(+), explaining how Li(+) catalyzes
CC       Ca(2+) exchange. In contrast to other members of the family its
CC       function is independent of K(+). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15060069,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:20018762, ECO:0000269|PubMed:22829870,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:23056385}.
CC   -!- ENZYME REGULATION: Strongly inhibited by zinc.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Mitochondrion inner membrane
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:20018762}; Multi-pass membrane protein
