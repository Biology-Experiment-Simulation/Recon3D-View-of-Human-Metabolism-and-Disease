CC   -!- FUNCTION: Enzyme which catalyzes the acetylation of polyamines.
CC       Substrate specificity: norspermidine = spermidine >> spermine >
CC       N(1)-acetylspermine > putrescine. This highly regulated enzyme
CC       allows a fine attenuation of the intracellular concentration of
CC       polyamines. Also involved in the regulation of polyamine transport
CC       out of cells. Acts on 1,3-diaminopropane, 1,5-diaminopentane,
CC       putrescine, spermidine (forming N(1)- and N(8)-acetylspermidine),
CC       spermine, N(1)-acetylspermidine and N(8)-acetylspermidine.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16455797, ECO:0000269|PubMed:17516632}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Acetyl-CoA + an alkane-alpha,omega-diamine =
