CC   -!- DISEASE: Pulmonary alveolar microlithiasis (PALM) [MIM:265100]:
CC       Rare disease characterized by the deposition of calcium phosphate
CC       microliths throughout the lungs. Most patients are asymptomatic
CC       for several years or even for decades and generally, the diagnosis
CC       is incidental to clinical investigations unrelated to the disease.
CC       Cases with early-onset or rapid progression are rare. A
CC       'sandstorm-appearing' chest roentgenogram is a typical diagnostic
CC       finding. The onset of this potentially lethal disease varies from
CC       the neonatal period to old age and the disease follows a long-
CC       term, progressive course, resulting in a slow deterioration of
CC       lung functions. Pulmonary alveolar microlithiasis is a recessive
CC       monogenic disease with full penetrance.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16960801}. Note=The disease is caused by
CC       mutations affecting the gene represented in this entry.

CC       formation of a SLC34A2-ROS1 chimeric protein that retains a
CC       constitutive kinase activity. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12661006}.
