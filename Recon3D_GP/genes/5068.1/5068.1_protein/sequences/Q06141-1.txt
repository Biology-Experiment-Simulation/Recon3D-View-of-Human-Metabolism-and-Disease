CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Secreted. Note=Found in the apical region of
CC       pancreatic acinar cells.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Highly expressed in epidermal keratinocytes of
CC       psoriasis patients (at protein level). Constitutively expressed in
CC       intestine. Low expression is found in healthy pancreas.
CC       Overexpressed during the acute phase of pancreatitis and in some
CC       patients with chronic pancreatitis. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:1469087,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:22727489}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Appears in pancreatic juice after induction of
CC       pancreatic inflammation.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The EPN motif is essential for recognition of the
