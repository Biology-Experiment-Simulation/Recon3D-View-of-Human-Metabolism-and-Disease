CC   -!- FUNCTION: Repressor of translation initiation that regulates EIF4E
CC       activity by preventing its assembly into the eIF4F complex:
CC       hypophosphorylated form competes with EIF4G1/EIF4G3 and strongly
CC       binds to EIF4E, leading to repress translation. In contrast,
CC       hyperphosphorylated form dissociates from EIF4E, allowing
CC       interaction between EIF4G1/EIF4G3 and EIF4E, leading to initiation
CC       of translation. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q13541}.
