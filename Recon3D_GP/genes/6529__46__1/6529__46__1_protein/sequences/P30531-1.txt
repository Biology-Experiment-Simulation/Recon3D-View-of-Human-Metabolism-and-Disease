CC       plasma membrane and in a subset of intracellular vesicles.

CC   -!- DISEASE: Myoclonic-atonic epilepsy (MAE) [MIM:616421]: A form of
CC       epilepsy characterized by myoclonic-atonic and absence seizures,
CC       appearing in early childhood. Patients have delayed development
CC       before the onset of seizures and show varying degrees of
CC       intellectual disability following seizure onset.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:25865495}. Note=The disease is caused by
CC       mutations affecting the gene represented in this entry.
