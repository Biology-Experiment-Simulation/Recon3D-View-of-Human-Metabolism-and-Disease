CC   -!- FUNCTION: Mediates the side-chain deamidation of N-terminal
CC       glutamine residues to glutamate, an important step in N-end rule
CC       pathway of protein degradation. Conversion of the resulting N-
CC       terminal glutamine to glutamate renders the protein susceptible to
CC       arginylation, polyubiquitination and degradation as specified by
CC       the N-end rule. Does not act on substrates with internal or C-
CC       terminal glutamine and does not act on non-glutamine residues in
CC       any position. Does not deaminate acetylated N-terminal glutamine.
CC       With the exception of proline, all tested second-position residues
CC       on substrate peptides do not greatly influence the activity. In
CC       contrast, a proline at position 2, virtually abolishes deamidation
CC       of N-terminal glutamine. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q80WB5}.
