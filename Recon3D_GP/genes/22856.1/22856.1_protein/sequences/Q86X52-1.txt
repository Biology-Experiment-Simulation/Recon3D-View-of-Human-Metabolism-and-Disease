CC   -!- FUNCTION: Has both beta-1,3-glucuronic acid and beta-1,4-N-
CC       acetylgalactosamine transferase activity. Transfers glucuronic
CC       acid (GlcUA) from UDP-GlcUA and N-acetylgalactosamine (GalNAc)
CC       from UDP-GalNAc to the non-reducing end of the elongating
CC       chondroitin polymer. Involved in the negative control of
CC       osteogenesis likely through the modulation of NOTCH signaling.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11514575, ECO:0000269|PubMed:21129727}.
