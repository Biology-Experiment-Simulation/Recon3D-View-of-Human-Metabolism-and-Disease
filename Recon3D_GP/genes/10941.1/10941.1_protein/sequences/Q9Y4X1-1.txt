CC   -!- FUNCTION: UDP-glucuronosyltransferases catalyze phase II
CC       biotransformation reactions in which lipophilic substrates are
CC       conjugated with glucuronic acid to increase water solubility and
CC       enhance excretion. They are of major importance in the conjugation
CC       and subsequent elimination of potentially toxic xenobiotics and
CC       endogenous compounds. Active on odorants and seems to be involved
CC       in olfaction; it could help clear lipophilic odorant molecules
CC       from the sensory epithelium. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19858781}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: UDP-glucuronate + acceptor = UDP + acceptor
CC       beta-D-glucuronoside.
