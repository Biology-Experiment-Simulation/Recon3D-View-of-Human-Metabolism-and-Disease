CC   -!- FUNCTION: UCP are mitochondrial transporter proteins that create
CC       proton leaks across the inner mitochondrial membrane, thus
CC       uncoupling oxidative phosphorylation. As a result, energy is
CC       dissipated in the form of heat. May play a role in the modulation
CC       of tissue respiratory control. Participates in thermogenesis and
CC       energy balance.
