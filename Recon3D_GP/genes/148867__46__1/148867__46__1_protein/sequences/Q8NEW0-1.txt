CC   -!- FUNCTION: Seems to facilitate zinc transport from the cytoplasm
CC       into the Golgi apparatus. Partly regulates cellular zinc
CC       homeostasis. Required with ZNT5 for the activation of zinc-
CC       requiring enzymes, alkaline phosphatases (ALPs). Transports zinc
CC       into the lumens of the Golgi apparatus and the vesicular
CC       compartments where ALPs locate, thus, converting apoALPs to
CC       holoALPs. Required with ZNT5 and ZNT6 for the activation of TNAP
CC       (By similarity). {ECO:0000250, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15276077,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:15994300}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homooligomer.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Golgi apparatus, trans-Golgi network
CC       membrane {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12446736}; Multi-pass membrane
CC       protein {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12446736}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Increased intracellular zinc level, resulting from
CC       extracellular zinc supplementation, do not induce any up- or down-
CC       regulation of gene expression. Up-regulated by zinc depletion.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15276077}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the cation diffusion facilitator (CDF)
CC       transporter (TC 2.A.4) family. SLC30A subfamily. {ECO:0000305}.
