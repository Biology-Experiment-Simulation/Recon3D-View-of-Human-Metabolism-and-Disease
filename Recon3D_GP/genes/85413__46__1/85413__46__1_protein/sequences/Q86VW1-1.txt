CC   -!- FUNCTION: High affinity carnitine transporter; the uptake is
CC       partially sodium-ion dependent. Thought to mediate the L-carnitine
CC       secretion mechanism from testis epididymal epithelium into the
CC       lumen which is involved in the maturation of spermatozoa. Also
CC       transports organic cations such as tetraethylammonium (TEA) and
CC       doxorubicin. The uptake of TEA is inhibited by various organic
CC       cations. The uptake of doxorubicin is sodium-independent.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12089149, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15963465}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Membrane {ECO:0000305}; Multi-pass membrane
CC       protein {ECO:0000305}. Cell membrane
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12089149}. Note=Detected in the plasma
CC       membrane of Sertoli cells and in the luminal membrane of
CC       epithelial cells in the epididymis.
