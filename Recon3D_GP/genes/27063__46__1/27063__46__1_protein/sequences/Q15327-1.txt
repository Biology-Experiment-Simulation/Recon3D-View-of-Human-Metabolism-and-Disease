CC   -!- DISEASE: Total anomalous pulmonary venous return (TAPVR)
CC       [MIM:106700]: Rare congenital heart disease (CHD) in which the
CC       pulmonary veins fail to connect to the left atrium during cardiac
CC       development, draining instead into either the right atrium or one
CC       of its venous tributaries. This disease accounts for 1.5% of all
CC       CHDs and has a prevalence of approximately 1 out of 15'000 live
CC       births. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18273862}. Note=The disease may be
CC       caused by mutations affecting the gene represented in this entry.
