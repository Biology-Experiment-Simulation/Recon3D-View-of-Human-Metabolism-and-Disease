CC       glycosaminoglycan biosynthesis. Substrates include asialo-
CC       orosomucoid (ASOR), asialo-fetuin, and asialo-neural cell adhesion
CC       molecule. Requires sphingomyelin for activity: stearoyl-
CC       sphingomyelin was the most effective, followed by palmitoyl-
CC       sphingomyelin and lignoceroyl-sphingomyelin. Activity was
CC       demonstrated only for sphingomyelin with a saturated fatty acid
CC       and not for that with an unsaturated fatty acid, regardless of the
CC       length of the acyl group. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:O35789}.
