CC   -!- DISEASE: Thiamine metabolism dysfunction syndrome 2, biotin- or
CC       thiamine-responsive type (THMD2) [MIM:607483]: An autosomal
CC       recessive metabolic disorder characterized by episodic
CC       encephalopathy, often triggered by febrile illness, presenting as
CC       confusion, seizures, external ophthalmoplegia, dysphagia, and
CC       sometimes coma and death. If untreated, encephalopathies can
CC       result in permanent dystonia. Brain imaging may show
CC       characteristic bilateral lesions of the basal ganglia.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15871139}. Note=The disease is caused by
CC       mutations affecting the gene represented in this entry.
