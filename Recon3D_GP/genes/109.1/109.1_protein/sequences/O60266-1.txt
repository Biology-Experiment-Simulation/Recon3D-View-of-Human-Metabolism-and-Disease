CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the formation of the signaling molecule cAMP

CC       cascades triggered by odorant receptors via its function in cAMP

CC       for normal sperm motility and normal male fertility. Plays a role
CC       in regulating insulin levels and body fat accumulation in response
CC       to a high fat diet. {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q8VHH7}.
