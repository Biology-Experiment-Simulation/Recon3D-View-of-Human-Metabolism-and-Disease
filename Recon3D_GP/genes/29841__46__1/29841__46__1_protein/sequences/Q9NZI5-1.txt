CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transcription factor involved in epithelial development.
CC       Binds directly to the consensus DNA sequence 5'-AACCGGTT-3'
CC       (PubMed:12175488, PubMed:18288204). Important regulator of DSG1 in
CC       the context of hair anchorage and epidermal differentiation,
CC       participates in the maintenance of the skin barrier. There is no
CC       genetic interaction with GRHL3, no functional cooperativity due to
CC       diverse target gene selectivity during epithelia development (By
CC       similarity). Isoform 1 may function as an activator and isoform 2
CC       as a repressor in tissues where both forms are expressed
CC       (PubMed:12175488). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q921D9,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12175488, ECO:0000269|PubMed:18288204}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer, also forms heterodimers with GRHL2 or GRHL3.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12175488, ECO:0000269|PubMed:12549979}.
