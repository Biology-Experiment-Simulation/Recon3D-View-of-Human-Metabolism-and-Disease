CC       retinol deficiency. Lack of normal levels of retinol impairs the
CC       visual cycle leading to night blindness at early stages; prolonged
CC       deficiency may lead to retinal degeneration. Additionally, retinol
CC       deficiency may result in dry skin, increased susceptibility to
CC       infection and acne (PubMed:23189188).

CC       Ocular abnormalities like opacities of the cornea and lens,

CC       also be present. Ocular colobomas are a set of malformations
