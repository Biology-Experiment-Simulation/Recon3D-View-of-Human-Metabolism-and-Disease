CC   -!- FUNCTION: Major role in the synthesis of nucleoside triphosphates
CC       other than ATP. The ATP gamma phosphate is transferred to the NDP
CC       beta phosphate via a ping-pong mechanism, using a phosphorylated
CC       active-site intermediate. Through the catalyzed exchange of gamma-
CC       phosphate between di- and triphosphonucleosides participates in
CC       regulation of intracellular nucleotide homeostasis
CC       (PubMed:10799505). Binds to anionic phospholipids, predominantly
CC       to cardiolipin; the binding inhibits its phosphotransfer activity
CC       (PubMed:18635542, PubMed:23150663). Acts as mitochondria-specific
CC       NDK; its association with cardiolipin-containing mitochondrial
CC       inner membrane is coupled to respiration suggesting that ADP
CC       locally regenerated in the mitochondrion innermembrane space by
CC       its activity is directly taken up via ANT ADP/ATP translocase into
CC       the matrix space to stimulate respiratory ATP regeneration
CC       (PubMed:18635542). Proposed to increase GTP-loading on dynamin-
CC       related GTPase OPA1 in mitochondria (PubMed:24970086). In vitro
CC       can induce liposome cross-linking suggesting that it can cross-
CC       link inner and outer membranes to form contact sites, and promotes
CC       intermembrane migration of anionic phosphoplipids. Promotes the
CC       redistribution of cardiolipin between the mitochondrial inner
CC       membrane and outer membrane which is implicated in pro-apoptotic
CC       signaling (PubMed:18635542, PubMed:17028143, PubMed:23150663).
