CC   -!- FUNCTION: Dual-specificity phosphatase involved in DNA damage
CC       response. Essential regulator of the G2 DNA damage checkpoint:
CC       following DNA damage, translocates to the nucleus and
CC       dephosphorylates FZR1/CDH1, a key activator of the anaphase
CC       promoting complex/cyclosome (APC/C). Dephosphorylates SIRT2 around
CC       early anaphase. Dephosphorylation of FZR1/CDH1 activates the
CC       APC/C, leading to the ubiquitination of PLK1, preventing entry
CC       into mitosis. Preferentially dephosphorylates proteins modified by
CC       proline-directed kinases. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17488717,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:18662541, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9367992}.
