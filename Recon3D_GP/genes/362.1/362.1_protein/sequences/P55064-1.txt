CC   -!- FUNCTION: Forms a water-specific channel. Implicated in the
CC       generation of saliva, tears, and pulmonary secretions. Required
CC       for TRPV4 activation by hypotonicity (PubMed:16571723). Together
CC       with TRPV4, controls regulatory volume decrease in salivary
CC       epithelial cells (PubMed:16571723). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16571723}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with TRPV4; the interaction is probably
CC       indirect and regulates TRPV4 activation by hypotonicity.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16571723}.

CC       {ECO:0000255}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: Aquaporins contain two tandem repeats each containing
CC       three membrane-spanning domains and a pore-forming loop with the
CC       signature motif Asn-Pro-Ala (NPA).
CC   -!- DISEASE: Keratoderma, palmoplantar, Bothnian type (PPKB)

CC       PPKB is frequently complicated by fungal infections.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:23830519}. Note=The disease is caused by
CC       mutations affecting the gene represented in this entry.
