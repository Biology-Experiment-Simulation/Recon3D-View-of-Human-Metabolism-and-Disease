CC   -!- FUNCTION: Sulfotransferase that utilizes 3'-phospho-5'-adenylyl
CC       sulfate (PAPS) as sulfonate donor to catalyze the sulfate
CC       conjugation of estradiol and estrone. May play a role in the
CC       regulation of estrogen receptor activity by metabolizing free
CC       estradiol. Maximally sulfates beta-estradiol and estrone at
CC       concentrations of 20 nM. Also sulfates dehydroepiandrosterone,
CC       pregnenolone, ethinylestradiol, equalenin, diethylstilbesterol and
CC       1-naphthol, at significantly higher concentrations; however,
CC       cortisol, testosterone and dopamine are not sulfated.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11884392}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: 3'-phosphoadenylyl sulfate + estrone =
CC       adenosine 3',5'-bisphosphate + estrone 3-sulfate.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11884392}.

CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12782487}.
