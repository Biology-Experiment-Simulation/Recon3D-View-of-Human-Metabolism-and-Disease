CC       intermediate in biosynthesis of 7-alpha,25-dihydroxycholesterol
CC       (7-alpha,25-OHC), an oxysterol that acts as a ligand for the G
CC       protein-coupled receptor GPR183/EBI2, a chemotactic receptor for a
