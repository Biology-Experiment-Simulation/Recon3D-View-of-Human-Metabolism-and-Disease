CC   -!- FUNCTION: Responsible for the 2-O-dephosphorylation of xylose in
CC       the glycosaminoglycan-protein linkage region of proteoglycans
CC       thereby regulating the amount of mature glycosaminoglycan (GAG)
CC       chains. Sulfated glycosaminoglycans (GAGs), including heparan
CC       sulfate and chondroitin sulfate, are synthesized on the so-called
CC       common GAG-protein linkage region (GlcUAbeta1-3Galbeta1-3Galbeta1-
CC       4Xylbeta1-O-Ser) of core proteins, which is formed by the stepwise
CC       addition of monosaccharide residues by the respective specific
CC       glycosyltransferases. Xylose 2-O-dephosphorylation during
CC       completion of linkage region formation is a prerequisite for the
CC       initiation and efficient elongation of the repeating disaccharide
CC       region of GAG chains. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:24425863}.
CC   -!- BIOPHYSICOCHEMICAL PROPERTIES:
CC       pH dependence:
CC         Optimum pH is 5.8. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:24425863};
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with B3GAT3; the interaction increases the 2-

CC       linkage region formation in a B3GAT3-mediated manner.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:24425863}.
