CC   -!- FUNCTION: Component of the GPI-GlcNAc transferase (GPI-GnT)
CC       complex in the endoplasmic reticulum, a complex that catalyzes
CC       transfer of GlcNAc from UDP-GlcNAc to an acceptor
CC       phosphatidylinositol, the first step in the production of GPI-
CC       anchors for cell surface proteins. May act by regulating the
CC       catalytic subunit PIGA. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16162815}.
CC   -!- PATHWAY: Glycolipid biosynthesis; glycosylphosphatidylinositol-
CC       anchor biosynthesis.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with the GPI-GnT complex composed of PIGA,

CC       Does not interact with Ras proteins.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16162815}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Endoplasmic reticulum membrane

CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16162815}.
