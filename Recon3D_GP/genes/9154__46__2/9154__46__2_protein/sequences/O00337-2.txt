CC   -!- FUNCTION: Sodium-dependent and pyrimidine-selective. Exhibits the
CC       transport characteristics of the nucleoside transport system cit
CC       or N2 subtype (N2/cit) (selective for pyrimidine nucleosides and
CC       adenosine). It also transports the antiviral pyrimidine nucleoside
CC       analogs 3'-azido-3'-deoxythymidine (AZT) and 2',3'-dideoxycytidine
CC       (ddC). It may be involved in the intestinal absorption and renal
CC       handling of pyrimidine nucleoside analogs used to treat acquired
CC       immunodeficiency syndrome (AIDS). It has the following selective
CC       inhibition: adenosine, thymidine, cytidine, uridine >> guanosine,
CC       inosine.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane; Multi-pass membrane protein.
