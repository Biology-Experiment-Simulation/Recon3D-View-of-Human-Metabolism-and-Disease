CC   -!- FUNCTION: Serine palmitoyltransferase (SPT). The heterodimer
CC       formed with LCB1/SPTLC1 constitutes the catalytic core. The
CC       composition of the serine palmitoyltransferase (SPT) complex
CC       determines the substrate preference. The SPTLC1-SPTLC3-SPTSSA
CC       isozyme uses both C14-CoA and C16-CoA as substrates, while the
CC       SPTLC1-SPTLC3-SPTSSB has the ability to use a broader range of
CC       acyl-CoAs without apparent preference.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19416851}.
