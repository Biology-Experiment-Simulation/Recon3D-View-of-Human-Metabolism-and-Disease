CC   -!- FUNCTION: Critical component of the visual transduction cascade,
CC       controlling the calcium concentration of outer segments during
CC       light and darkness. Light causes a rapid lowering of cytosolic
CC       free calcium in the outer segment of both retinal rod and cone
CC       photoreceptors and the light-induced lowering of calcium is caused
CC       by extrusion via this protein which plays a key role in the
CC       process of light adaptation. Transports 1 Ca(2+) and 1 K(+) in
CC       exchange for 4 Na(+). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10608890}.

CC   -!- DISEASE: Night blindness, congenital stationary, 1D (CSNB1D)

CC       of electroretinogram (proportionally reduced a- and b-waves).
CC       Patients have visual acuity within the normal range and no
