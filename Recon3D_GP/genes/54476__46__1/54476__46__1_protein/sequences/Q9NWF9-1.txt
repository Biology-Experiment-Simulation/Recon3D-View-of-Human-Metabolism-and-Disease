CC   -!- FUNCTION: Isoform 1 acts as an E3 ubiquitin ligase, which accepts
CC       ubiquitin from specific E2 ubiquitin-conjugating enzymes, and then
CC       transfers it to substrates promoting their degradation by the
CC       proteasome. Promotes degradation of TRAF3, TLR4 and TLR9.
CC       Contributes to the regulation of antiviral responses. Down-
CC       regulates activation of NF-kappa-B, IRF3 activation and IFNB
CC       production. Isoform 3 inhibits TNF and IL-1 mediated activation of
CC       NF-kappa-B. Promotes TNF and RIP mediated apoptosis.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15107846, ECO:0000269|PubMed:19893624}.
